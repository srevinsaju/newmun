What is NewMUN

NewMUN is an interactive platform for students to research on topics, debate on an issue, and learn how to make solutions for the today's problems.

Research

NewMUN requires active research on the delegates' part to an issue given among the council and gather points to show the validity of a preferred solution.

Discussion

Facts are brought together or in opposition of other facts by other delegates to come to a conclusion in the NewMUN Council

Resultion

Delegates get the cance to form solutions for the Issues in their Council, just like a UN Council takes for the World

Model United Nations, for students!

An MUN conference is a great experience and a must-be-explored event.

  • Meet people and places – Get to know different nations and widen your friends circle
  • Public speaking – Have a fear facing audience? MUN conferences are the best in helping anyone and everyone overcome their fear of public speaking
  • Become acquainted with global issues – Your opinion may contribute to solve the problems that affect our planet today
  • Diplomacy and negotiation skill – MUNs are diplomatic simulations; respect and co-operation are fundamentals of the debate, helping us build our individuality

Why NewMUN?

NewMUN is one of the best organized model united nations in the world. Here's why.

By the Students

NewMUN is as democratic as all UN Institutions. Right from Organizing, Chairing, Planning, Video Editing, Graphic Designing, Web Hosting is completely done by Students.

Of the Students

NewMUN has students representing countries or people and fight for their issues and concerns, and come to a solution.

For the Students

NewMUN is organized for all students, from Grade 7 to Grade 12.
